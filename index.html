    夢想Dream

    小時候的夢想總是想要環遊世界，長大之後才發現太不實際，因為根本不可能，世界太大錢太少，時間也不夠呀！
    而我現在的夢想是能夠找到一份我自己熱愛的工作，做著自己喜歡做的事，
    然後每年都能夠出國旅遊，多學一點別國語言，算是朝環遊世界邁進。
    然後希望自己能夠成為一個更好的自己，做事都要盡全力，勇敢去冒險去生活去挑戰。

    When I was little, my dream was always been to travel around the world.
    But as I grow up, I found this dream not practical.
    The world is way too big, and I have too little money, and not enough time, either.
    Now, my dream is to find a job that I love, doing the thing I like, and be able to go abroad every year.
    Also I want to be a better person, do everything with my whole heart, not afraid of taking risk.
    Go make a wonderful life.
